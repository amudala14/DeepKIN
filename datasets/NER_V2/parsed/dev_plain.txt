Ubwiswe Delta bwagaragaye bwa mbere mu Buhinde mu Mpera za 2020 bushyirwa ku isonga mu kwandura vuba ndetse bitekerezwa ko ari bwo ntandaro ya impfu na ubwandu bya COVID - 19 bibarirwa mu bihumbi bimaze iminsi biboneka muri icyo gihugu buri masaha 24 .

Magingo aya hagaragazwa ko abarenga 91 % mu banduye COVID - 19 mu Bwongereza basanganywe Delta .
Ntabwo haranzurwa icyaba gitera uko kwandura ku muvuduko uri hejuru kwa Delta , gusa Biorxiv iheruka kumurika inyigo igaragaza ko utunyangingo twayo twihuriza hamwe kugira ngo tubone imbaraga zo kwinjira mu mubiri neza .
